About Swim101

Swim101 is an iOS app designed for swimmers of all levels. Track your pool and open-water workouts, log sets and distances, monitor your progress over time, and stay motivated to reach your swimming goals - all from your iPhone.

All data is stored securely on your device. No account registration is required, and no personal data is transmitted to any server.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

1. Overview

Swim101 ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Swim101 iOS application handles information. By using Swim101, you agree to the practices described below.

2. Data We Collect

Swim101 does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All information you enter in the app - including workout logs, distances, times, notes, and settings - is stored exclusively on your device using Apple local storage technologies (Core Data / UserDefaults).

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact details
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Health or fitness data beyond what you manually enter
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or analytics
  • Cookies or web-tracking technologies

3. HealthKit

If you grant Swim101 permission to read or write data to Apple Health (HealthKit), that data remains under your control via iOS Settings - Health - Data Access. We do not share HealthKit data with third parties or use it for advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

Swim101 does not integrate third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or crash reporting services that transmit personal data off your device.

5. Data Security

Because all data lives on your device, it is protected by your device built-in security (passcode, Face ID / Touch ID, and iOS encryption). We recommend keeping your iOS version up to date for the best protection.

6. Children Privacy

Swim101 is suitable for users of all ages. Because we do not collect any personal data, there is no special risk to children. Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise device usage as appropriate.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact
